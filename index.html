Biography

I'm a senior at Mongol Aspiration International School. I enjoy solving challenging problems in physics and astrophysics. Previously, I studied at Logarithm, where I became an avid physics enthusiast. Spreading knowledge is a big mission of mine, so feel free to contact me at telmuun@mais.edu for physics-related inquiries. For everything else, try telmuun@gmail.com
